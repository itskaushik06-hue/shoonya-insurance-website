## ARTICLE TEMPLATE & STRUCTURE

### Article Structure (1,000-1,500 words)

```
1. Introduction (100-150 words)
   - Hook: Start with a question or statistic
   - Problem statement
   - Value proposition
   - What reader will learn

2. Section 1: Background/Context (200-250 words)
   - Explain the concept
   - Why it matters
   - Current landscape

3. Section 2: Key Points/Details (300-400 words)
   - Main point 1 with explanation
   - Main point 2 with explanation
   - Main point 3 with explanation

4. Section 3: Practical Benefits/Examples (250-300 words)
   - Real-world examples
   - Benefits breakdown
   - Use cases

5. Section 4: Implementation/Next Steps (150-200 words)
   - Actionable steps
   - How to get started
   - Call-to-action (CTA)

6. Conclusion (100-150 words)
   - Summarize key takeaways
   - Reinforce value
   - Final CTA

7. FAQ Section (100-150 words)
   - 3-5 frequently asked questions
   - Direct answers
   - Links to relevant products
```

## ARTICLE WRITING GUIDELINES

### SEO Best Practices for Articles

1. **Keyword Integration:**
   - Primary keyword in title
   - Primary keyword in first 100 words
   - Primary keyword in at least one subheading
   - 2-3% keyword density throughout
   - Use keyword variations naturally
   - LSI keywords throughout

2. **Length & Structure:**
   - Minimum 1,000 words (prefer 1,200-1,500)
   - Bulleted lists for scannability
   - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
   - Subheadings every 300-400 words
   - One image per 400 words
   - Conclusion with CTA

3. **Internal Linking:**
   - 2-4 internal links per article
   - Use descriptive anchor text
   - Link to product pages
   - Link to related articles
   - Link to main solutions pages
   - Place first link in first 300 words

4. **External Links:**
   - 1-2 high-authority external sources
   - Link to relevant industry resources
   - Add `rel="noopener noreferrer"` to external links

5. **Readability:**
   - Grade 8-10 reading level
   - Active voice preferred
   - Short sentences
   - Clear explanations
   - Actionable content

### Metadata Template

```
Title: [Primary Keyword]: [Benefit/Question] | Shoonya
Meta Description: [Benefit explanation]. Learn [key point 1], [key point 2], and [key point 3] in this complete guide.
URL Slug: /article/[keyword-with-hyphens]
H1: [Article Title]
Focus Keyword: [Primary keyword]
LSI Keywords: [3-5 related keywords]
Internal Links: [List of 2-4 pages to link]
Image Count: [Target 2-3 images minimum]
```
